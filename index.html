About Us

AKAP is a small business, designed to meet the needs, wants and expectations of the ever-changing world, by giving a competitive product, which is eco-friendly, stylish, and affordable.We chose this company name because of the Filipino word “Akap” which means hug or to express affection. We want to embrace the nature with youby offering our eco-friendly styled belt.We often hug our loved ones for them to feel comfortable and be relieved from any stressful moments, and that is what we want them to feel when they become our partners in terms of protecting our environment. AKAP connotes the feeling of safety and security. Literally, it is the simplest way to comfort our partners and customers.

Products

See Our Featured Products.

AKAP Enterprise is offering an Eco Belt which means, Eco-friendly belt. It’s made from abaca fibers in which Northern provinces of Philippines is rich with this kind of materials. As of now Philippines is the largest supplier of this fibers worldwide. As a company that promotes eco-friendly products we are promoting the indigenous products of the Philippines to support them locally. This product promotes the culture of Aeta Community in Handicraft Industry and maintaining of the production for nontoxic or non-harmful raw materials that is being used in process. This will also give them chances to have profit as we know that life as a farmer are hard
